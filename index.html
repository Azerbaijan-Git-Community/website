Strategic Plan

The Path to 5,000,000

A structured 5-stage national movement for digital transformation.

Stage 1

Launch & Onboarding

GitHub Azerbaijan established, chapters formed, pilot programs initiated, and GitHub Global onboarding.

Stage 2

Expansion

Launch of OSS projects, initial government integrations, and expansion into regional chapters.

Stage 3

National Hub

Establishment of the National OSS hub, unlocking full perks, and launching the GitHub certification track.

Stage 4

Scaling & AI

AI integrations (Digital Cemetery), country-wide participation, and mass skill assessments.

Stage 5

The Goal Achieved

5,000,000 pushes and a strong GII improvement.
